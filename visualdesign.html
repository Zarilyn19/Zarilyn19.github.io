Pop Art Celebrities

The way I changed the colors for my subject’s face is by clicking on the highlighted rectangle underneath that opens the color picker and I choose the colors I wanted. The tools that I used to complete my portrait are mostly using new layers to get different colors. I also used these tools by choosing a color to fill my image behind. I chose this selfie because I wanted to try it on a picture that has me in it. The emotions that this selfie is showing is excitement because the colors I have chosen I think represent excitment.
