Pop Art Celebrities

The way I changed the colors for my subject’s face is by clicking on the highlighted rectangle underneath that opens the color picker and I choose the colors I wanted. Kaleidoscopes

This project was made in an adobe shop. The steps that I used to achieve my kaleidoscope was opening my filtered image and duplicating the layers to edit the picture. My original image was different after applying all of these steps because it had multiple layers which also changed the original color. The results were that the colors had changed and the layers which also brings out the picture more.
